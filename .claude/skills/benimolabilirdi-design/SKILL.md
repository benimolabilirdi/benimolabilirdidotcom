## Quick orientation
- **Brand:** buruk ama sıcak (bittersweet but warm). A friend's voice, not an institution. Turkish, informal "sen".
- **Palette:** cream ground `#FAF3E8`, navy ink `#1E2A4A`, coral (tax/shock) `#E85D4A`, green (kept/dream) `#2E7D5B`, gold sparkle `#E8A33D`. Never a red-white flag composition.
- **Type:** Nunito (display/warm) + DM Sans (UI/numbers, tabular-nums for figures).
- **Motifs:** wavy underlines, ✨ sparkle (never confetti), count-up numbers, emoji as first-class icons, pill buttons, soft navy-tinted paper shadows.
- **Tokens** live in `tokens/` (linked via `styles.css`). **Components** in `components/` (namespace `window.BenimolabilirdiDesignSystem_717d58`). **Screens** in `ui_kits/`.
- Hard nos: political-party color layouts, anger iconography (fists/megaphones), corporate grey-blue fintech, stock photos, real brand logos.
